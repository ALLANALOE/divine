About Our Ministry

Divine Influence Ministry is a prophetic ministry dedicated to bringing God's word to nations and transforming lives through divine revelation.

Our Mission

To raise a generation of believers who walk in divine influence and prophetic authority, impacting nations with the gospel of Christ.

Our Vision

To establish prophetic centers worldwide where God's presence is tangible and lives are transformed through divine encounters.

Our Values

We value prayer, holiness, the prophetic, divine encounters, and the manifestation of God's power in everyday life.

Our Prophet

Meet Prophet Emmanuel Mwasinga, the visionary leader of Divine Influence Ministry

Prophet Emmanuel Mwasinga

Prophet Emmanuel Mwasinga is a servant of God with a strong prophetic mantle. Called from a young age, he has dedicated his life to the ministry of the Word and the demonstration of God's power.

With over 15 years in ministry, Prophet Emmanuel Mwasinga has impacted countless lives through his accurate prophetic ministry, deliverance sessions, and revelational teachings.

He is the founder and lead prophet of Divine Influence Ministry, with a vision to raise prophetic voices that will influence nations for Christ.
